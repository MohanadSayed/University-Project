Undergraduate Financial Aid

October Technological meets the full financial need of every admitted undergrad who qualifies for assistance. Tuition is covered for undergrads with family incomes under $150,000. Tuition, room, and board are covered for undergrads with family incomes below $100,000.

About Our University

Who We Are

What drives our faculty, students, and staff is a spirit of possibility, a legacy inherited from our pioneering founders who created October Technological in the memory of their only child to benefit other people's children. At October Technological , we learn from our past but focus on the future, continually looking for new ways to make the world a better place.

We believe strongly in the role that higher education plays in supporting a thriving society. Colleges and universities not only prepare students for their chosen path, but they help create the next generation of citizen leaders and problem solvers.
